Frequently Asked Question's

How do I use the hostel washing machine?


Watch Our Tutorial Video

Can I wash delicate items in the hostel washing machines?

It is advisable to hand wash delicate items. If you must use the machine, use a gentle cycle and place delicate items in a mesh laundry bag for protection.

What is the cost associated with using the washing machine in the hostel?

The use of washing machines is included in your hostel fees, and there is no additional cost.

Is laundry detergent provided, or should I bring my own?

You are responsible for bringing your own laundry detergent. The hostel does not provide it.

How often can I use the washing machine?

Atmost two times a week.

What should I do if the washing machine malfunctions?

Report about it , through visiting Support page.
